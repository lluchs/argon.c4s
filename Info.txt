What's new in 1.45 NET2?
- The spell "Camouflage" costs justs 14 manapoints again.
- There's a new scoreboard, in which you can see the relaunches of all players.
- The spell "Weaponcourse" was removed. Now there are sparks, which fall from above and which create a random object on bounce.
- Kills are counted now and also shown in the scoreboard. For each kill, you'll get some objects, for each third kill, you'll get an extra relaunch.

What's new in 1.42?
- The spell "Camouflage" costs 22 manapoints now and the stone hurts clonks now just a little bit.

What's new in 1.4?
- The scenario and the contained objects were translated into English.
- There is a relaunch delay of 2 seconds now.
- You can see your remaining relaunchs in the player information.
- The spells "Local force field" and "Spying" were deleted.
- The fireball can't shoot directly down anymore.
- The cooldown object works with effects now.
- Everything was adjusted to Clonk Endeavour.
- A lot of other changes and bugfixes.
